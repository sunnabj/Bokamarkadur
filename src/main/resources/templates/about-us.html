Project Bókamarkaður provides a platform for students to find used school books and offer their own in a convenient and user-friendly environment. They no longer have to scroll through multiple posts in facebook sale groups, but can now find what they‘re looking for quickly and easily, by using the search and categorization functions on the site. Users can request or offer available books by direct messaging.
